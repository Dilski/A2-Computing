Company Profile

W.W Brazell ltd was established in 1895 in the building we occupy today as a building contractor.

Dave Casey and Co. Electrical contractors was incorporated in the 1980’s and the electrical contracting aspect of the business has been developed since then.

We are a family run business currently employing approximately thirty electrical tradesmen undertaking all aspects of both private and contract work across the north east of England.

All work is carried out to meet BS 7671 17th edition (current) wiring regulations and are part P compliant.

We are members of construction line and the N.I.C.E.I.C (National Inspection Council for Electrical Installation Contracting) which is our governing body for more information click on the logo below

In recent years we have worked for most of the local authorities of housing associations across the region specialising mainly in social housing revites.
